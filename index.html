About Us

Welcome to Edwards Drive-In and Dashboard Dinner, home of the legendary handmade pork tenderloin, hand-dipped onion rings and homemade root beer. Edwards originally opened as a Dog'n Suds drive-in on August 10, 1957 and was owned and managed by Herb Edwards and his son Tom, which was later passed down to Tom's son and daughter, Jeff and Terri.
